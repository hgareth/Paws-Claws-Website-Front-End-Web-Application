Dog Care Guide

Taking care of a dog is a rewarding experience. Here are some general tips to ensure your furry friend stays happy and healthy.

Feeding

  • Provide a balanced diet with high-quality dog food.
  • Avoid feeding your dog human food, especially chocolate, grapes, and onions, as they can be toxic.
  • Ensure fresh water is always available.

Exercise

  • Take your dog for daily walks to keep them active and healthy.
  • Engage in playtime with toys or games like fetch.

Grooming

  • Brush your dog’s coat regularly to prevent matting and reduce shedding.
  • Trim their nails every few weeks.
  • Bathe your dog as needed, but not too frequently to avoid drying out their skin.

Health

  • Schedule regular vet check-ups and vaccinations.
  • Watch for signs of illness, such as lethargy, loss of appetite, or unusual behavior.
